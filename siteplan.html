Site Name

White Water Rafting Co.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to advertise the services of White Water Rafting Co. In addition to providing a web presence that will generate greater visibility for White Water Rafting Co., the site also has the function of collecting payments and scheduling reservations for rafting expeditions.

Site Audience

The target audience is individuals with a smart phone and a budget of about $150. Reasons for visiting the site could include answering the question, what exciting experiences are available in my area? Also, how can I prepare for a rafting experience? Another audience is those looking for family and group activities. On a larger scale, camps and retreats may find out how to utilize the equipment and services of White Water Rafting Co.
