What Kinds of Books Do You Like?

Do you like books that are adventurous? Do you like books that are exciting and fun? Then you've come to the right place! The books here are full of colorful female leads who take charge of their own story! So dive right in to find your next great read and get inspired!

Now it's time to meet the books!

Caddie Woodlawn

Meet Caddie Woodlawn, a girl that loves to play with her older brothers and an outgoing and friendly kid growing up in the 1900s. Join her as she befriends her Native American neighbors and shows those around her what it means to be a brave pioneer!

The Hero and The Crown

This is a classic tale of girl-takes-on-the-world! Join Aerin Dragon-Killer as she transforms from palace waife to immortal legend!

Edge of Extinction

This action-packed book is about a girl and her friend who break free from the Compound in order to deliver important information that could save the world. But does she have enough time?

Race to the Sun

This book is full of adventure! Nezoni Begay has to escape from school to hop on a train far away to save her dad from a monster.

The Wizards of Once

Wish and Xar are supposed to be sworn enemies. When a threat bigger than their differences comes back into the world, they have to help each other and save the world before it's too late!
