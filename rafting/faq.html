Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring?

Just a water bottle, some 6sunscreen, and a willingness to have fun! We'll supply the rest!

How do I get there?

We're located at 777 Canyon Run Drive. If you'd like directions, give us a call!

Do you supply the gear?

Absolutely! We have 4, 6, and 8-seater rafts, depending on the size of your group. We also carry all the necessary life vests and helmets for your adventure.

Is there an age minimum?

Our Float the River trip is a wonderful fit for ages 3 and up. For our more advanced trips, it is recommended that participants are at least 10 years old and confident swimmers.

Do you provide a guide?

Yes, all our trips include certified guides to join you on your river trip.

How much does it cost?

The price varies depending on which trip you choose. Our Float the River trip lasts about 1.5 hours and costs $60 per person. Our White Waters trip lasts 2 hours and costs $80 per person. Our High Adventure trip lasts 3 hours and costs $120 per person.

Do you offer lessons?

Yes! Our trained guides are available to schedule private or group lessons. Call to schedule a time today!

How can I document my rafting adventure?

Our river trips include the option of having your photograph taken at certain points along the river run. Our guides will tell you when you get close to one of our prepositioned cameras. Copies of your photographs can be ordered after your trip.

Is there cell phone reception?

We have excellent cell phone reception at the rafting lodge. We discourage the use of cell phones while on the raft.
